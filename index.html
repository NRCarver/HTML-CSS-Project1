What is a Life Hack?

Time Saving

A good life hack should be a quicker, more efficient way of doing something.

Cost Effective

A good life hack should be cost effective, so it is useful to all.

Different

A good life hack should challenge the status quo and the way things have previously been done. We want novel ideas to change the world!

What are Life Hacks for?

Life hacks can be anything from how to keep beer cold, quick and easy DIY or tips to help manage colleagues

The scope is massive and we encourage you to submit all of your own life hacks to share with the community
